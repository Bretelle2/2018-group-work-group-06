Dark

Have you ever
wondered what blind
people dream?

If you have never
seen anything in your
life, you'd dream
with your hearing,
your smell and your
touch.

Today is a day like
any other.

You wake up, go about
your morning routine,
get ready to go out.

Today is a normal
day, but you have to
go somewhere.

Somewhere important.

You feel the warmth
of the sun on your
skin, the noise of
the city around you.

Suddenly, your dog
pulls, his leash
escaping your hand.

You have to find him,
and quickly, before
you get lost.
